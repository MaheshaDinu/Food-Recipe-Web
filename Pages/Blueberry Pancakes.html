Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Buttermilk
  • 1 large Egg
  • 2 tbsp Unsalted Butter, melted
  • 1 cup Blueberries
  • Maple Syrup, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients:

    In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  2. Add Wet Ingredients:

    In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; the batter should be slightly lumpy.

  3. Fold in Blueberries:

    Gently fold the blueberries into the batter.

  4. Cook the Pancakes:

    Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.

  5. Serve:

    Serve the pancakes warm with maple syrup.
