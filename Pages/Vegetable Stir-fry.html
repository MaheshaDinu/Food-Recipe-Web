Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 200g Broccoli Florets
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, sliced
  • 1 Yellow Bell Pepper, sliced
  • 1 Carrot, julienned
  • 100g Snap Peas
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Seeds
  • Cooked Rice, to serve

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Vegetables:

    Wash and chop all the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Cook the Vegetables:

    In a large wok or skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and saut√© until fragrant. Add the broccoli, peppers, carrot, and snap peas, and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.

  3. Add the Sauce:

    Pour the soy sauce and sesame oil over the vegetables and toss to coat. Cook for another 2 minutes.

  4. Serve:

    Sprinkle sesame seeds over the stir-fry and serve immediately over cooked rice.
