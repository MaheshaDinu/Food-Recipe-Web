Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken Breast, cubed
  • 200g Plain Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Tikka Masala Paste
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Ginger, minced
  • 200ml Tomato Purée
  • 200ml Coconut Milk
  • Fresh Coriander, chopped (for garnish)
  • Salt & Black Pepper, to taste
  • Basmati Rice, to serve

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken:

    In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, tikka masala paste, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken cubes and marinate for at least 30 minutes, preferably overnight.

  2. Cook the Chicken:

    Heat a large skillet over medium heat and cook the chicken until browned on all sides. Remove from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Make the Sauce:

    In the same skillet, sauté the onion, garlic, and ginger until softened. Stir in the tomato purée and coconut milk, bringing the mixture to a simmer.

  4. Combine and Serve:

    Return the chicken to the skillet and cook for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve over basmati rice.
