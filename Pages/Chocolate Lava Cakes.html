Chocolate Lava Cakes

Ingredients:

  • 200g Dark Chocolate, chopped
  • 100g Butter
  • 100g Sugar
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • Powdered Sugar, for dusting
  • Vanilla Ice Cream, to serve

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ramekins:

    Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F). Grease 4 ramekins with butter and dust with flour to prevent sticking.

  2. Melt the Chocolate and Butter:

    In a heatproof bowl, melt the dark chocolate and butter together, either in the microwave or over a double boiler. Stir until smooth and glossy.

  3. Mix the Batter:

    Whisk the sugar into the chocolate mixture until dissolved. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract and flour until just combined.

  4. Bake the Cakes:

    Divide the batter evenly among the prepared ramekins. Place them on a baking tray and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center is still soft.

  5. Serve:

    Let the cakes cool slightly before inverting onto plates. Dust with powdered sugar and serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
